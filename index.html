Introduction

An overview of the Blakely Golf App — what it does and who it's for.

Blakely Golf App (BGA) ⛳

BGA is a comprehensive golf game management platform designed to digitize and enhance the experience of BGA golf formats such as Calcutta. The app enables organizers and players to manage courses, tee sheets, games, scoring, and betting seamlessly.

C. Home Screen (Agenda Tab)

Your personalised view of all upcoming games and events activity.

New User? This Tab Will Be Empty at First

If you have just signed up — as a Player or as an Organiser — and have no games or events yet, the app automatically redirects you to the General Tab instead of the Agenda Tab. This is expected behaviour. The Agenda Tab becomes populated once:

  • An Organiser creates a game and adds you as a player, or
  • You create your first game as an Organiser.

The Agenda Tab is the personalised activity hub inside the Home Screen. It shows all layers (players) and organisers associated with your upcoming or active games and events in one consolidated view — so you always know what's happening and who's involved.

What You'll See in the Agenda Tab

All Layers (Players)

See every player enrolled in your upcoming games and events at a glance.

Organisers

View the organiser(s) managing each game or event you are part of.

Upcoming Events

All scheduled games and events are listed in chronological order.

Quick Navigation

Tap any agenda item to jump directly into the game or event overview.

Agenda Tab — User Flow by Role

Organiser

After sign-up you land on the General Tab (no events yet). Once you create your first game via Create Game, your Agenda Tab will populate with that event and all enrolled players.

Player

After sign-up you land on the General Tab. Once an Organiser creates a game and adds you to a tee sheet, the Agenda Tab will show the game details, the organiser's name, and your fellow players.

Tip

Returning users with active games will be taken directly to the Agenda Tab on app open — giving you an instant overview of all players and organisers for your next event or game.

Three Payment States

Each bet in the Pay bottom sheet has one of three states: Pay (you owe — pay now), Pending (you won — send reminder to the person who owes you), or Settled (equal reward — no payment needed).

01
Ledger Page View
Navigate to the Ledger Tab from Game Details. The ledger shows the financial summary — Point Values Or Skin Values, Skode pool, and Junk pool values, and a list of all foursomes with each player's name and email. This is the complete settlement overview for the game.
02
Tap Pay — Bottom Sheet
The Pay bottom sheet slides up showing a full breakdown of all side bets Won / Lost for this player. Each bet line shows the bet type, outcome, and amount. Three action states are possible depending on the result:
PAY You Owe — Pay Now

You lost one or more bets. The Pay Now button is shown. Tap it to confirm payment and mark the bet as settled in the ledger.

PENDING You Won — Send Reminder

You won the bet and are owed money. The Pending button is shown. Tap it to send a reminder notification to the person who owes you — prompting them to pay.

SETTLED Equal Reward — No Payment

Both players won equally — rewards cancel out. The bet is marked Settled automatically. No payment is required and no action is needed from either player.

03
Tap View — Winning Amount
Tap the View button next to any player to open their individual settlement summary. This shows the winning or owing amount for that player — how much they are owed by others or how much they owe — based on all bets, Skode, Junk, and Skins outcomes.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the BGA app.

What is the BGA app?
The Blakely Golf App (BGA) is a comprehensive golf game management platform for BGA formats such as Calcutta. It allows organizers to create games, manage courses, configure bets, and track scoring — all from a mobile device.
Do I need to add bets before adding scores?
Yes — always add bets before adding scores. This ensures the system can correctly calculate outcomes and payouts based on the bet conditions you've configured.
What if my golf course isn't in the app?
If your course isn't found after allowing location access, tap Import Course to search by name or club. If it still doesn't appear, you can manually create a course using the Create Course option.
What is the Game Wizard vs Legacy Game Create?
The Game Wizard is a recommended step-by-step guided flow that walks you through 9 configuration steps. Legacy Game Create provides a single form page where you fill in all details at once. The wizard is recommended for first-time users.
Can I create multiple games in the same event?
Yes. After saving a game, tap "Create Game for Another Day" to add another game within the same event — using the same tee sheet or a new one.
What bet types are supported?
BGA supports Win/Lose and Over/Under bet types. You can bet on Skode, Junk, individual holes, the overall game, and more. Each bet can be configured with specific players/teams, outcome conditions, and amounts.
How do I view results and the ledger?
From the Game Details screen (accessed via View Game), you can navigate to the Results tab for standings and the Ledger tab for financial tracking of all bets and payouts.
